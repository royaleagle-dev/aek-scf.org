Why The Foundation?

Why?

"The thought of the foundation came up to immortalize Adedotun so that this can be of help to others with Sickle Cell Anemia (providing financial help when it is required) and the society at large. This is also to sensitize people on Sickle Cell Anemia. Jesus Died that we might live so in the same vane, we pray that Adedotun's death will not be in vain and would help others with the disease have a better and prolonged life."

Adedotun Emiloluwafe Kuyebi

Adedotun spent 26 years on earth and would have been 27 years old today, Wednesday 19th May, 2021. On several occasions, we have ben advised to let him marry as soon as possible so that he would leave a child or children behind even if the Lord calls him hoe early. We felt that decision would have been selfish and self-centered as his wife would have become a widow at a tender age and the child/children fatherless. Moreover, he himself was bent on finishing his studies before going into marriage. He took his studies more important than even his health. It was because he had an assignment in school that he refused to be admitted in the hospital early enough, who knows, maybe or maybe not he would still be alive.

Objectives of the Organisation

Adedotun Emiloluwafe Kuyebi Sickle Cell Foundation (AEKSCF) is a non-profit, non-governmental organisation (NGO) with the sole purpose of supporting research, education and funding of individuals, families of those who are impacted by sickle cell disease

Mission of the Organisation

The mission of the Adedotun Kuyebi Sickle Cell Foundation is to enlighten the world about Sickle Cell Anemia and to be a connecting link in finding a cure Our Mission is to

"improve the whole lives of those living with Sickle Cell Anemia across the ation by employing innovative strategies to deliver impactful advocacy initiatives and live-enhancing programs, while supporting new research & therapies to cure the disease"

It is a humanitarian effort channeled towards saving lives of people living with Sickle Cell Anemia and helping in the education of the society to prevent its occurrence.
